Walter Hartwell White 

About his life 

Walter is a Chemistry teacher, but his real passion is traveling for his special job. He is manufacturing methamphetamine and drug dealing with his former student Jesse Pinkman. So he takes that to his advantage and he is ready for more adventures. Mr. White has a private life with his lovely wife at a small house near Eindhoven. 

Age: 50, City: Eindhoven 

Occupation: Drug manufacturer Co-founder of Gray Matter Technologies High school chemistry teacher Car wash cashier, proprietor, and manager 

Type of traveling 

He rarely uses air transport as it is expensive and has a lot of police. He prefers trains, buses and sometimes he is even hitchhiking. 

  • Lack of people who are willing to help him with transportation. 

  • Finding cheap tickets online, Mr. White prefers unplanned travel so it is hard for him to find and plan all his journeys. That's why sometimes he uses Jesse. 

  • Waiting time on different train stations, when Walter travels by train, he usually needs to take steps to switch the train which makes him uncomfortable. He also is losing a lot of time by doing this and attracts attention. 

  • Train tickets are the biggest problem for Walter because they are trackable by the police and he often buys multiple train tickets to go in only one direction which is expensive. 

 

Problems faced now 

Mr. White is frequently traveling alone, he has great communication skills which allow him to meet new people and to get them know especially if he is armed. Walter does not care about traveling methods, as long as it is cheap and safe he is fine with that.
